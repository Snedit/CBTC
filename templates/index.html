Event Planner 360 is an internship project made by Soham De. This platform will allow users to host, create, join and plan different events like birthdays, parties, hackathons, etc.

Users can also book different services like decorators, caterers, location managers, all from this platform, while keeping a track of all their past, current and future events in their dashboard.

You can think it as a website inspired from Devfolio

Simply login and you're good to go. The rest of the process is quite intuitive.
